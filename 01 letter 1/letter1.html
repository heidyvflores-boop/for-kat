The In-Between

for all the moments you are waiting for that seem galaxies away,
for all the things you've been carrying around
that your heart has been longing to finally say,
for all of the distances you've traveled
just to land on some bridge in The In-Between,
where you've played your part
and done all you can do,
and yet still, the song is not resolving,
I hope, even here, you can feel the river
running wild beneath: a steady flow
despite it all
reminding you
of what still breathes.
the landscape still breathes,
and you are still breathing, too...
even before the waiting
and these distances make any sense to you.
there is still movement here,
and it might not look like you'd expect.
and yet, the breaths you take here
still carry movement for what comes next.
so when you find yourself
on a bridge somewhere
in The In-Between,
may you always be the one
who pauses and notices
the river running beneath.
feel the river running beneath
right here, in The In-Between...
there is more to this landscape than it seems.
your heart has not seen all that it will see.
— MHN
